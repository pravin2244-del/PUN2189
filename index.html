Popular Places of the Future

Shaniwar Wada Holographic Museum

The historical fortress is now a multi-layered holographic museum, preserving the Maratha Empire's legacy through interactive light projections and AI-guided tours.

Dagdusheth ganapti Mandir

The magnificent, jewel-encrusted idol of Lord Ganesha is a breathtaking sight, radiating peace and spiritual energy. Beyond its beautiful architecture and grandeur, the temple is a beacon of hope, founded in the late 19th century after a personal tragedy by a sweetmeat vendor. It continues to be a symbol of faith and community, with its trust actively involved in numerous charitable and social welfare initiatives."

Aga Khan Palace

A serene oasis amidst the bustling future, the palace stands as a monument to peace and non-violence. It's now an international cyber-diplomacy hub, using virtual reality to host global peace talks and cultural exchanges.

About This Project

This website is a speculative glimpse into what Pune might become in the year 2189. It is a creative project imagining a future where technology enhances, rather than replaces, the city's rich history and culture. All locations and descriptions are fictional and based on historical places in Pune.
